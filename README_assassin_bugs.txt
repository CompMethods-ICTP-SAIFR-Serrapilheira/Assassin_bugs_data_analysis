### Article: "Trophic allometry in a predator that carries corpses of its prey"
### DOI: 10.1111/btp.13148
### Journal: Biotropica 
### by Victor & Costa-Pereira (2022)

# The file "data_assassin_bugs.csv" was used to do data analysis in R.

# These are the variables used in the file: 
ID = assassin bug individual
mean_vol_ant = mean volume of a single ant (in pixels)
mean_vol_backpack = mean volume of the backpack (in pixels)
num_ants = number of ants present in the backpack
pred_size = body lengh of the predator (in pixels)
prey_size = mean head lengh of an ant (in pixels)
ppsr = predator prey-size ratio
